🌊 SeaSafe — Fortis Hackathon Project
🚀 Overview

SeaSafe is an AI-powered marine safety and emergency response platform designed to improve safety for fishermen, coastal communities, rescue teams, and marine authorities.

The project focuses on providing:

🌦️ Real-time weather and sea condition alerts
📍 Live GPS-based location tracking
🚨 Emergency SOS system
🤖 AI-powered risk prediction and safety assistance
📡 Fast communication during critical marine situations

SeaSafe aims to reduce accidents at sea and help save lives through smart technology and rapid emergency response systems.

💡 Problem Statement

Many fishermen and small marine workers face dangerous situations due to:

Sudden weather changes
Lack of emergency communication
Poor tracking systems
Delayed rescue operations
Limited access to real-time safety information

SeaSafe solves these challenges by integrating AI, location services, and emergency management into a single platform.
